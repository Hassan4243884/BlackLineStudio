Shining Light comes to Black Line Studio

For body piercing fans, Black Line Studio is pleased to announce we will be carrying yet another high-quality body jewelry line—Shining Light.

Shining Light’s reputation has made incredible waves across the industry for providing high-quality jewelry at affordable prices. Established in 1998, this San Diego-based wholesaler has been leading the way in a sea of body jewelry options due to its lifetime guarantee on every product they sell.

With a home-base in San Diego, California, Shining Light is held to the strictest body jewelry standards in the US. When you purchase their jewelry, you can rest assured it will be well-crafted from only the safest, medical-grade metals and elements.

Black Line Studio, your number one rated tattoo, piercing, and laser treatment boutique in Toronto, is now carrying a full line of Shining Light products. In order to appease everyone’s tastes, we will be stocking this high-end body jewelry line in LVM surgical steel, titanium, and gold. Gold jewelry will be available in both white gold and yellow gold and range between 14k, 16k, and 18k designs.

Regardless of what style you are looking for, Black Line Studio has exactly what you need to tailor your look to suit your personality. From studs and rings to barbells and dermal studs, there is something for every taste and piercing.

Maria Tash

Maria Tash’s line of body jewellery isn’t your average body jewellery—it’s a reimagining for those with a more fashion-conscious wearer. Featuring high-quality metals, precious gems, and unique layouts, the Maria Tash line has been top of the industry for over 21 years and has been seen on clients such as Scarlett Johansson, Beyonce, Charlize Theron, and Gwyneth Paltrow.

These high-quality, highly-sought after jewellery lines are now featured and available at Black Line Studio. Between custom tattoos, piercings, and superior jewellery lines, Black Line Studio has exactly what you need to tailor your look to suit your personality.
