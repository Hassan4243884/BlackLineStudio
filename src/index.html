Unique Body Art Boutique in Toronto

Established in 2006, Black Line Studio is an innovative concept which incorporates the essentials of the art world through the combination of an upscale tattoo shop, body piercing and laser treatment boutique with the most exclusive selection of art, jewelry, and after-care revitalizers. Read more

  • Offering an exclusive collection of art for exhibit and acquire, Black Line Studio showcases its’ resident artist team’s full range of artistic talent and features several other Canadian Artists. Fashion jewelry carried at the boutique consists of carefully selected lines which enable customers to express their individual style with unique pieces.
  • Black Line Studio’s downtown location is set in one of the most vibrant and eclectic neighbourhoods in the city situated in the heart of King Street West, while the uptown location is in Canada’s most prestigious outdoor mall – Shops at Don Mills.
  • Black Line Studio’s wide range of clientele varies from Toronto residents and globetrotters to A-List celebrities. All are welcome to express themselves at Black Line Studio!
