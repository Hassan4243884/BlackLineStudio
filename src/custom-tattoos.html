It’s Not Just a Tattoo

If you’re looking for a high quality tattoo and an enjoyable experience, Black Line Studio is the place to go. Get your ink done by a professional artist and turn your body into a work of art. Our staff is committed to ensuring that you leave happy with the final product and back you up with the knowledge of how to take care of your new tattoo.

We offer a variety of tattoo samples for you to choose from varying from small symbols to large elaborate tattoos. If you have something specific in mind and want a custom tattoo we do that too! Whatever your reason for getting inked, our staff will make sure that you end up with a tattoo that you’ll love for a lifetime.

A Tattoo Just For You

If you’re looking to get inked but can’t decide on a design for your tattoo or are stuck for an idea then a custom tattoo is the way to go. You can work together with the artist to design a tattoo that you love or bring in a picture, sketch or design to follow.

However you choose to do it, you’ll end up with a personal tattoo that is 100% original, and no one else has. You’re life is a journey so why not tell your story by getting some amazing custom body art designed to tailor to your interests.
